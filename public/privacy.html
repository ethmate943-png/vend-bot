Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

1. Who we are

MoovMart helps vendors run WhatsApp-first storefronts with secure payments and basic automation. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our links, bots, and tools.

2. What we collect

We collect only the information we need to run your store and protect buyers and sellers.

  • Vendor details – WhatsApp number, business name, store code, category, location, delivery coverage, and settings you share with us during onboarding.
  • Buyer details – WhatsApp identifiers, messages sent to the bot, basic delivery details, and transaction history related to orders.
  • Payment & transaction data – Amounts, status, references, and payout details from our payment partners (for example, Paystack).
  • Technical data – Log data like IP address, device information, and basic analytics events used to keep the service stable and secure.

3. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your MoovMart store and store code.
  • To route and respond to WhatsApp messages using our AI assistant.
  • To process, verify, and reconcile payments and payouts.
  • To prevent fraud, resolve disputes, and keep buyers and vendors safe.
  • To improve product performance and user experience.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your data. We only share information with:

  • Payment processors – to process buyer payments, escrow, and payouts.
  • Infrastructure providers – secure hosting, databases, and monitoring tools we use to run MoovMart.
  • Regulators or law enforcement – only when required by law or to prevent fraud and abuse.

5. Data retention

We keep transaction and vendor data for as long as necessary to comply with financial, tax, and regulatory requirements, and to protect buyers and vendors from fraud and chargebacks. Non-essential logs and analytics data are kept for shorter periods.

6. Your choices

  • Vendors – can update business information, pause their store, or request account closure via WhatsApp support.
  • Buyers – can request access to, or deletion of, limited personal data we store tied to their transactions, subject to legal retention requirements.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls, and monitoring. No system is perfectly secure, but we design MoovMart with safety and trust as defaults.

8. Third‑party services

MoovMart integrates with WhatsApp, payment processors, and other infrastructure providers. Their own privacy policies apply in addition to this one. We aim to work only with reputable, security-conscious partners.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, notify vendors via WhatsApp or email.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle data, you can reach our support team through your MoovMart WhatsApp bot or the contact details provided in your onboarding materials.
